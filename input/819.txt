Here we report the discovery of a partial homininmandible with teeth from the Ledi-Geraru research area, Afar RegionalState, Ethiopia, that establishes the presence of Homo at 2.80 to 2.75Ma. This specimen combines primitive traits seen in earlyAustralopithecus with derived morphology observed in later Homo,confirming that dentognathic departures from the australopith patternoccurred early in the Homo lineage.
