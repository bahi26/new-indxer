class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">Hushangazhi</a> (Industry in English) zvinoreva nvzimbo dzinoitirwa mabasa ekugadzira zvinhu; kushumira nenzira dzakasiyana, nokukohwa zviwanikwa zviri muvhu kana muamasango. Tiri pahushangazhi zvakanaka kutsanangura kupiwa kwemazita matsva ari pano.</p><p><br /></p><div id="toc" class="toc"><div class="toctitle" lang="sn" dir="ltr"><h2>Contents</h2></div><ul><li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-1"><a href="#Mazita_ezvinowanikwa_pahushangazhi"><span class="tocnumber">1</span> <span class="toctext">Mazita ezvinowanikwa pahushangazhi</span></a></li><li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-2"><a href="#Mipanda_yeHushangazhi"><span class="tocnumber">2</span> <span class="toctext">Mipanda yeHushangazhi</span></a></li><li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-3"><a href="#Zvinopinda_muHushangazhi"><span class="tocnumber">3</span> <span class="toctext">Zvinopinda muHushangazhi</span></a></li><li class="toclevel-1 tocsection-4"><a href="#Hudaviri_neHutongi_muHushangazhi"><span class="tocnumber">4</span> <span class="toctext">Hudaviri neHutongi muHushangazhi</span></a></li></ul></div><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Mazita_ezvinowanikwa_pahushangazhi">Mazita ezvinowanikwa pahushangazhi</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hushangazhi&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=1" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Mazita ezvinowanikwa pahushangazhi">chinja</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hushangazhi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Mazita ezvinowanikwa pahushangazhi">edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2><p><b>Kushangazhika</b> (Industrious)- izwi iri rinoreva kushanda nesimba, munhu achiita mabasa mazhinji. Uyu munhu anoshangazhika, munhu anoshanda asina unyope.</p><p><b>Mushangazhi</b> (Industrialist) - iri izita rapiwa kududza muridzi akatanga kambani yake. Pari zvino hatina izwi rinodudza kuti <i>Industrialist</i> tisina kuti muzvinabhizinisi.</p><p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">Hushangazhi</a> (Industry) - iri izita rapiwa kuti rireve nzvimbo dzose dzakavakirwa kuti vashandi vange vachisangana vachiita mabasa akasiyana-siyana. Muenzaniso ungapiwa ndewekuti muHarare tine Dunhu re<a class="mw-selflink selflink">Hushangazhi</a> reWillowvale - (Willowvale Industrial Area).</p><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Mipanda_yeHushangazhi">Mipanda yeHushangazhi</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hushangazhi&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=2" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Mipanda yeHushangazhi">chinja</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hushangazhi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Mipanda yeHushangazhi">edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2><p><b>Hushangazhi Hwekutanga</b> (Primary Industry) - iri iboka re<a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a> rinosanganisira: kurima mbesa, miti nemichero; kupfuya mhuka; kuredza hove, kuchera mabwe nesimbi zvichingodaro. <i>Hushangazhi</i> hwekutanga hunechekuita nemabasa anokohwa zviwanikwa zviri muvhu nezvinokura muvhu. Mimwe mienzaniso <i>yeHushangazhi Hwekutanga</i> yatinowana muZimbabwe ndeiyi: kurima nzimbe, chibage, nedonje; kuchera <a href="/wiki/Mugodhi" title="Mugodhi">mugodhi</a> ye <a href="/wiki/Ndarama" title="Ndarama">ndarama</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mhangura" title="Mhangura">mhangura</a>, <a href="/wiki/Zeng%27e" title="Zeng&#39;e">zeng'e</a>, platinum, <a href="/wiki/Marasha" title="Marasha">marasha</a>(coal) neChrome; kurima michero yemaranjisi, kurima miti yemapuranga.</p><p><b>Hushangazhi Hwepiri</b> (Secondary Industry) - iri iboka reHushangazhi rinoshandura zvibereko zvinobuda <i>muHushangazhi hwekutanga</i> kuita zvinhu zvinoshandiswa mazuva ose. Zvibereko zve<i>Hushangazhi Hwepiri</i> zvinoshandiswa mudzimba, mumabasa uye zvinogona kushanda mune mamwe mapoka <i>eHushangazhi Hwepiri</i>. Mimwe mienzaniso yatinowana muZimbabwe ndeiyi: kugadzira shangu; kugaya chibage kuita hupfu; kuveza midziyo yemumba; kuruka machira zvichingodaro.</p><p><b>Hushangazhi Hwetatu</b> (Tertiary Industry) iri iboka reHushangazhi rinoshumira mumabasa akasiyana-siyana; zvibereko zveHushangazhi uhwu hazvisiri zvinhu zvinobatika asi kubatsira kuwanikwa nekufambiswa kwezvinhu zvinobatika. Marudzi <i>eHushangazhi Hwetatu</i> anosanganisira maBhangi; Zvitoro; Kufambiswa kwezvinhu pamwe nevanhu; Insurance; mabasa eHurumende, matare emhosva; maHotera; nzvimbo dzemafaro nedzekushanya, zvichingodaro. Iri boka reHushangazhi rine chekuita nekusvitsa <a href="/wiki/Hushumiri" title="Hushumiri">Hushumiri</a> (Service) kuvanhu uye kune mamwe mapoka e<i>Hushangazhi</i>.</p><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Zvinopinda_muHushangazhi">Zvinopinda muHushangazhi</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hushangazhi&amp;veaction=edit&amp;section=3" class="mw-editsection-visualeditor" title="Edit section: Zvinopinda muHushangazhi">chinja</a><span class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hushangazhi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Zvinopinda muHushangazhi">edit source</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2><p>Chikamu chino chinotsanangudza zvinodikanwa kuti Hushangazhi hunge huchifamba nenzira yakanaka inounza budiriro. Zvinopinda mu<a class="mw-selflink selflink">Hushangazhi</a> (Industrial Inputs) zvinosanganisira: zvinhu zvinobatika; zvivakwa; zvirongwa zveHurumende; zvirongwa zvekuwanikwa kwemari; kuchengetedzwa kwemitemo; kutsiva mari inorasika munjodzi; zvichingodaro.</p><p><a href="/w/index.php?title=Vambe,_huvambe&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Vambe, huvambe (page does not exist)">Vambe, huvambe</a> (Capital)- iri zita rapiwa rinoreva mari inounganidzwa ne<b>mushangazhi</b> kutanga bhizinisi kana <a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a>. Zita iri ribatawo zvakare zvimwe zvinodiwa kutanga bhizinizi, zvinoti: michina, nzvimbo yokushandira, zvivakwa, zvichingodaro. Huvambe hunounganinzwa nenzira dzakasiyana dzinoti: zvikwereti zvema Bhangi, mari yakachengetedzwa, uye kutsvaka mari pa<a href="/w/index.php?title=Musika_wemari&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Musika wemari (page does not exist)">musika wemari</a> (Stock Exchange).</p><p><b>Nzvimbo yekushandira</b> (Land) - mhando dzose dze<a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a> dzinoda nzvimbo yekushandira ingava: minda, masango, zvivakwa, mahofisi kana mafakitari.</p><p><a href="/w/index.php?title=Magetsi&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Magetsi (page does not exist)">Magetsi</a> (Electricity)- Magetsi akakosha kana tatarisana ne<a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a> hwanhasi nokuti michina mizhnji inosundwa nesimba nemagetsi. Zvakanyanya kukosha kuva nemagetsi akabaka nguva dzose kuti makambani asarase mari achibhadhara vashandi vakatarira michina isiri kushanda. </p><p><b>Vanhu vane Hunyanzvi</b> (Skilled People)- mabasa anobatwa mu<a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a> anodaidzira hunyanzwi hwepamusoro, hungava hunyanzwi hunodzidziswa mu<a href="/w/index.php?title=Zvikoro_zvetatu&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Zvikoro zvetatu (page does not exist)">zvikoro zvetatu</a> (tertiary education) kana kuti hunyanzwi hunowanikwa pakambani. Makambani nehurumende vanoshandisa mari yakawanda kudzidzisa hunyanzwi zvemabasa akasiyana-siyana.</p><p><b>Michina yeHushangazhi</b> (Plant, Machines, Equipment)- kune mhando dzakasiyana-siyana dzemichina zvichienderana nebasa riri kushandwa. Michina mizhinji inosundwa nemagetsi uye inotengwa nemari zhinji. Pari zvino michina mizhinji inoshandiswa mu<a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a> muZimbabwe, inotengwa kunze kwenyika zvikuru kunyika dziri mhiri kwemakungwa - nyika dzekuEurope neAsia.</p><p><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ruzivo_RweHumhizha&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Ruzivo RweHumhizha (page does not exist)">Ruzivo RweHumhizha</a> (Technology)- kuti <a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a> huvepo panofanirwa kuve nehumhizha hwacho hwekugadzira kana kushumira nenzira inobuditsa zvibereko zviri kutengwa nevanhu. Sokuti kuchera ndarama panodiwa kunge paine <a href="/w/index.php?title=Humizha&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Humizha (page does not exist)">humizha</a> hunosanganisira: kudhuura matombo pasi pemugodhi, kutakura chitofu (ore) kubva mumugodhi, mhandambwe dzinopwanya nekutseta chitofu (crushers &amp; mills), nezvimwe zvakawanda zvinosvitsa pakuve nechidhinha chendarama. </p><p><b>Mari yeZvirikwereti</b> (Bank Loans)</p><p><b>Mari yeTsivatsaona</b> (Insurance)- Tsivatsaona chirongwa chinobatsira vemabhizinisi munguva yokunge nzvimbo dzavo dze<a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a> dzarasikirwa nemari kuburikidza nenjodzi dzinoitika mu<a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a>. Vamabhizinisi vanoripa muripo kuKambani dze<a href="/wiki/Tsivatsaona" title="Tsivatsaona">Tsivatsaona</a> (Insurance) mwedzi wega-wega; musi unoitika tsaona munzvimbo ye<a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a>, makambani eTsivatsaona anotsiva hukoshi hwechinhu chinenge charasika mutsaona. Izvi zvinobatsira kuti vemabhizinisi vakwanise kudzoka pakushanda zvakare mushure metsaona. Kambani dzeTsivatsaona dzinogamuchira mudeme-mudeme kubva kumabhizinisi akawanda, izvi zvichibatsira kuti pave nehomwe yemari inoshandiswa musi unoitika tsaona kune avo vanenge vakaita mudeme-mudeme. </p><p>Zvirongwa zve<a href="/wiki/Tsivatsaona" title="Tsivatsaona">Tsivatsaona</a> zvinofukidzira kurasikirwa kungaitwa mutsaona dzingava dzemotokari, dzemichina, kutsva nemoto, kukuvara kwevashandi pabasa, rufu nezvimwe zvinenge zvakabvumiranwa kunzi zvive zvakafukidzwa. Kune mitemo yehurumende inopa zvisungo zvokuti vashandi vange vakafukidzirwa pasi pe<a href="/wiki/Tsivatsaona" title="Tsivatsaona">tsivatsaona</a>. Zvakare kune mhando dzemitoro isingatenderwe kufamba pamigwagwa isina kutererwa ne<a href="/wiki/Tsivatsaona" title="Tsivatsaona">tsivatsaona</a>.</p><p><b>Mitemo yeNyika</b> (Legislation, Policies, Regulations) - Mitemo yenyika yakakosha zvikuru kana tatarisana nesarudzo yokuti muzvinabhizinisi ovaka nzvimbo yo<a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a> kupi. Mitemo yenyika inodudza kuti zviri nyore sei kuvamba bhizinisi munzvimbo dzakasiyana-siyana. Zvinhu zvinotariswa nevanovamba mabhizinisi zvinosanganisira: mitero inoripwa nemakambani; mitemo inochengetedza kodzero dzokunge munhu ari muzvinabhizinisi; mitemo inotonga kuburitswa nekuchengetwa kwemari yokunze kwenyika; mitemo inotaura nezvekuchengetedzwa kwezviwanikwa nenharaunda iri kushandira <a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a>, zvichingodaro. </p><p><b>Kutakurwa kweNhumbi</b> (Transport) - hakuna nzvimbo inganzi inowanikwa zvinhu zvose zvinodikwa kushandiwa muhushangazhi, kana kuti inowanikwa musika yokutengesera zvabuda muhushangazhi. Apa ndipo pazvinokosha kuti pange pane migwagwa, njanji, ndege nedzimwe mhando dzokufambisa nadzo mbatya nevanhu, kuunza kunzvimbo dze<a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a> uye kusvitsa kumusika yakasiyana-siyana. Tichoitarisa nyika yedu yeZimbabwe tinoona <a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a> hwakasimbiswa nekuvapo kwenjanji, nemigwagwa.</p><p><a href="/wiki/Suwo" class="mw-redirect" title="Suwo">Suwo</a> (Port of Entry kuEnglish) inzvimbo inopindwa nayo nevanhu pamwe nenhumbi. Takatarisana ne<a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a>, mukova inzvimbo yakatenderwa pamutemo wenyika kuti ipindwe nayo nevanhu kana nhumbi. Pamitemo yenyika pamukova iyoyi ndipo chete panotenderwa kupinda kwevanhu nenhumbi. <a href="/wiki/Suwo" class="mw-redirect" title="Suwo">Suwo</a> reBeitbridge ndiro rinopinda nenhumbi dzakawanda dzinopinda muZimbabwe.</p><p><br /><b>Kuchengetedza Mitemo</b> (Law Enforcement) - vemabhizinisi vanofara kuziva kuti nzvimbo dze<a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a> dzavo dzinge dzakachengetedzwa pasi pemitemo uye kuti mbatya dzavanofambisa dzinge dzakachengetedzawo. Izvi zvinovapa kuva nechikuvimba kuti vane rutsigiro pasi pemitemo kana musi wasvika wokuti pane zvinoda kunotongwa kumatare edzimhosva.</p><p><b>Zvirongwa ZveHutano</b> (Health Programs) - Kuvapo kwezvipatara kunosimbisawo <a class="mw-selflink selflink">hushangazhi</a> zvikurusei takatarisana nekuti vashandi vave nekuchengetedzwa kwehutano.</p><p><a href="/wiki/Bundutso" title="Bundutso">Bundutso</a> (Processes)- pasi pechikamu cheRuzivo rwehumhizha, <b>Bundutso</b> zvinoreva zvirongwa zvakatsikiswa zvinoshandiswa mukushandura nhumbi dzinopinda muhushangazhi kuti dzive zvibereko zvinokwanisa kutengeseka. Somuenzaniso, <a href="/wiki/Gasi" title="Gasi">gasi</a> reAmmonia rinogadzirwa kuchishandiswa Bundutso ra<b>Haber</b> (Haber process).
